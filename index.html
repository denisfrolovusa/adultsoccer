FAQ

Is this really adults-only?
Yes—participants must be 18+. These sessions are designed for adults, with adult pacing and contact expectations.
What skill level is this for?
Mixed levels. We structure drills with progressions and group players appropriately for games.
What should I bring?
Cleats, shin guards, water. If you have one: a light + dark shirt for teams.
Do you have goalkeepers?
Sometimes. If not, we run small goals or rotate keepers depending on the session format.
Can I get a refund?
If a session is canceled by us, you get a credit automatically. For other cases, contact us and we’ll work it out.
